About Us

At Keloke Dominican Restaurant & Food Truck, we invite you to embark on a flavorsome expedition that transcends borders and takes your taste buds straight to the heart of the Dominican Republic. Our restaurant is more than just a dining destination; it's a cultural immersion into the vibrant tapestry of Dominican life, where every dish tells a story and every bite carries the warmth of our island home.

Join us, where the rhythms of merengue blend with the sizzle of grilling meats, and the heart of the Dominican Republic beats in every dish. Come for the food, stay for the experience, and leave with a piece of our culture in your heart.
